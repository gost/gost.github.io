More Info

OGC SensorThings API

An Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC) standard providing an open and unified framework to interconnect IoT sensing devices, data, and applications over the Web. It is an open standard addressing the syntactic interoperability and semantic interoperability of the Internet of Things

LOWER OVERHEAD

It permits the proliferation of new high value services with lower overhead of development and wider reach

LOW RISK

It lowers the risks, time and cost across a full IoT product cycle

SIMPLIFY

It simplifies and accelerates the development of IoT applications without worrying about the protocols of the different IoT devices,
